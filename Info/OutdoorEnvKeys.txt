Keys in Environment3D
-------------------------------
LeftAlt - QuickWire
W - Move Forward
A - Move Left
S - Move Backward
D - Move Right
LeftShift - Move Faster
F - Place Cube in world
F1 - Enable Lighting - Need to add a variable to all shaders to toggle lighting
F2 - Toggle Wireframe Mode
F3 - Toggle PostProcessing
F4 - Toggle Fullscreen Mode
F5 - Toggle Tessellation
F6 - Descrease Camera Speed
F7 - Increase Camera Speed
F8 - Decrease Tessellation Factor
F9 - Increate Tessellation Factor

Keys in OutdoorEnvironment
-------------------------------
1 - Toggle Water
2 - Toggle between lake water and ocean
3 - Toggle Terrain
4 - Toggle Sky
5 - Toggle Sky Plane
6 - Toggle Water Fog
P - Raise Sea Level
O - Lower Sea Level
V - Raise Terrain Height
B - Lower Terrain Height
G - Increase Terrain Size and Regenerate Terrain Mesh
H - Decrease Terrain Size and Regenerate Terrain Mesh
T - Decrease Tessellation Minimum distance
Y - Increase Tessellation Minimum distance
U - Decrease Tessellation Maximum distance
I - Increase Tessellation Maximum distance
C - Clip Camera to terrain
K - Decrease Terrain Render Size
L - Icrease Terrain Render Size
N - Toggle Render Full Map
M - Toggle Extra Render Distance
Up - Move Point Light Forward
Down - Move Point Light Backward
Left - Move Point Light Left
Right - Move Point Light Right
E - Move Point Light Up
R - Move Point Light Down
